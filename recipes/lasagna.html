Lasagna

Description

Mouth watering lasagna garnished with basil leaves. Glistening in the sunlight.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • A meat or vegetable sauce
  • Ricotta or cottage cheese
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onions
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Boil noodles: Cook lasagna noodles until just tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make sauce: Sauté onions and garlic, add your meat or vegetables, then stir in tomato sauce. Simmer.
  3. Layer 1: Spread a bit of sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  4. Layer 2: Add a layer of noodles.
  5. Layer 3: Spread ricotta or cottage cheese over noodles
  6. Layer 4: Add more sauce, then sprinkle mozzarella and a bit of parmesan.
  7. Repeat layers: Continue layering until ingredients are used up, ending with sauce and cheeses on top.
  8. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 to 30 minutes. Uncover and bake another 10 to 15 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  9. Cool & serve: Let it rest 10 to 15 minutes before slicing.
