Lasagna

Description

Mouth watering cheesy quesadilla with you and your kids will love. It doesn't go down easy, if it ain't Cheesy.

Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas
  • Shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, etc.)
  • Butter or oil

Steps

  1. Heat pan over medium and lightly oil or butter it.
  2. Place tortilla, sprinkle with cheese, then top with another tortilla.
  3. Cook both sides until golden and cheese is melted (2 to 3 mins per side).
  4. Slice and eat hot.
