Lasagna

Description

Mouth watering garlic butter pasta that won't leave your mouth smelling like garlic. Great for a person on a diet.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Garlic (2 to 3 cloves)
  • Ricotta or cottage cheese
  • Butter (2 to 3 tablespoons)
  • Salt
  • Optional: Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water. Drain, saving a bit of the water.
  2. Saut√© garlic in butter until fragrant (1 to 2 mins)..
  3. Mix pasta with garlic butter. Add a splash of pasta water to coat.
  4. Optional: Sprinkle parmesan and serve hot.
